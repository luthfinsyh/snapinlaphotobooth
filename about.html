About us

Home  /  About us

We are a creative photobooth partner, focused on transforming your special moments into timeless memories. Our mission is to blend high-end photography with seamless digital experiences, ensuring every guest leaves with a smile and a masterpiece in their hands.

We are leading photobooth & entertainment partner Jakarta based

We use professional DSLR cameras and studio lighting to ensure every guest looks stunning. Our high-speed printers deliver lab-quality physical prints in seconds.

Every event is unique. We provide tailored photo templates, custom backdrops, and curated props that align perfectly with your brand or event theme.

Beyond physical prints, we offer instant digital sharing via QR codes and provide a private online gallery to access all high-resolution memories after the event.
